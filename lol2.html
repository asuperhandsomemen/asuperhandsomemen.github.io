K/DA

K/DA is a virtual K-pop girl group consisting of four themed versions of League of Legends characters: Ahri, Akali, Evelynn and Kaisa. American singers Madison Beer, Jaira Burns, and (G)I-dle members Miyeon and Soyeon provide the voices of Evelynn, Kai'Sa, Ahri, and Akali respectively, though the characters have also been voiced by other artists.


K/DA was developed by Riot Games, the company behind League of Legends, and unveiled at the 2018 League of Legends World Championship with an augmented reality live performance of their first song, "Pop/Stars". A music video of the song uploaded to YouTube subsequently went viral, surpassing 100 million views in one month, reaching 497 million views as of February 2022, and topping Billboard's World Digital Song Sales chart. This would be followed up with the release of an EP in 2020 entitled All Out, which featured 5 new songs including the pre-released single "The Baddest" and the lead single "More" which later received a music video that gained over 140 million views as of February 2022.
